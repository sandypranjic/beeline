Beeline is a small web-based app that provides individuals with Toronto’s live bike share information to help users make a beeline for their next destination!

Enter your starting location below and how many bikes you will need - it’s great to travel in groups! Afterwards, you’re going to need to park your bike, so don’t forget to enter your destination and the number of spots you will need to find the nearest available docks around your endpoint.
